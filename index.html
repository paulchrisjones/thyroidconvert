The calculator assumes that:

  1. T3 is four times as potent than T4. [Sources: 1, 2]
  2. Each 60 mg grain of Armour Thyroid, Thyroid-S and WP Thyroid contains 9 mcg of T3 and 38mcg of T4. [Sources: 1, 2]
  3. Each 60 mg grain of Thiroyd and ERFA contains 8 mcg of T3 and 35 mcg of T4. [Source]
  4. Each 65 mg grain of Nature-Throid and NP Thyroid contains 9 mcg of T3 and 38 mg of T4. [Source]
  5. Each 300 mg capsule of Natural Sources Raw Thyroid contains 8 mcg of T3 and 32 mcg of T4. [Source
  6. Each 150 mg capsule of Thyro-Gold contains 12.5 mcg of T3 and 50 mcg of T4. [Source]
  7. The brands Eltroxin, Euthyrox, Letrox, Levaxin, Lévothyrox, L-thyroxine, Thyrax, Thyrax Duotab, Thyrox, Thyronorm, Unithroid, Eutirox, Levoxyl, Synthroid, Tirosint, Thyrin, and Thyrolar are all bioequivalent with generic levothyroxine.
